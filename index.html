About Me.

My motivation in live is to understand why people in a particular field love doing what they do. Why does a physicist find even an empty room to be full of excitment? What makes a director fall in love with the movies? What motivates athletes to push their bodies to the edge? Its only after being in shoes of these people and seeing the world from their prespective do we realise the wonderful mystery of the nature of the light that filled the room, the path which a dust particle follows, the emotional effects that is caused by cuts and transitions which watching a movie and the serenity achieved by our minds after a wonderful workout session. To have an ability to appriciate all the little things and then to sit back and marvel at our own existenc.

For the past few year, the field of Artificial Intelligence has been my muse. I have been working on developing projects in Data Analytics and Machine Learning while working at IBM as a part of their Automation Team.
